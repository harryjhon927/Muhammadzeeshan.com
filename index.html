Frequently asked

Common questions about SEO & AI search

Direct answers to the questions founders, marketing leads, and AI engines ask most often.

What is GEO / AEO and why does it matter in 2026?

+

GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) and AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) are the practices of making your brand and content visible inside AI search engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google's AI Overviews. In 2026, a growing share of search queries never reach a traditional results page, the AI answers directly. If your content isn't structured for entity recognition, citations, and Q&A retrieval, you become invisible. GEO / AEO fixes that.

How long does SEO take to show real results?

+

Technical fixes can move rankings in 2 to 4 weeks. Content and authority work compounds over 3 to 6 months, with the strongest gains usually visible between months 6 and 12. Anyone promising "page one in 30 days" for competitive terms is selling a story. I work in 90-day cycles with clear milestones so you can see progress every month, not wait a year for a verdict.

Do you work with Shopify and WooCommerce stores?

+

Yes. Shopify SEO is one of my core specialties, collection page architecture, faceted navigation, app-bloat cleanup, structured data, and Liquid template optimization. WooCommerce, Magento, and headless setups are also in scope. I've handled Shopify migrations end-to-end without losing rankings, which is the part most agencies get wrong.

What's included in a free SEO audit?

+

A real audit, not a Lighthouse screenshot. You get a 30 to 45 minute call where I share-screen your Search Console, crawl your site live, identify the three biggest leaks (technical, content, or authority), and tell you whether SEO is even the right channel for you right now. No deck, no slides, no obligation, you walk away with a clear next step whether you hire me or not.

Do you offer Local SEO for USA UK and UAE businesses?

+

Yes, this is one of the most common engagements. Local SEO covers Google Business Profile optimization, citation building, review velocity, on-page schema, and location landing pages. I've delivered Local SEO across the USA, UK, UAE, Saudi Arabia, and Pakistan, single-location, multi-location, and service-area businesses included.

How do you optimize for ChatGPT Perplexity and Google AI Overviews?

+

Three layers. First, entity clarity, your brand, services, and people are unambiguously defined across your site, schema, and authoritative third-party sources. Second, content is structured in clean Q&A blocks AI engines can lift verbatim, with citations to credible sources. Third, off-site presence, Wikipedia where appropriate, knowledge graphs, and trusted publications, so AI engines repeatedly encounter your name in the right contexts.

Can you fix Google algorithm update penalties?

+

Yes. Algorithm-update recovery is a Tier 03 Custom engagement. The first week is diagnosis, isolating which update hit, which pages bled, and whether the cause was technical, content, link-based, or E-E-A-T related. From there I prioritize fixes by impact and timeline. Most recoveries take 2 to 4 months once the right fixes ship, but the diagnosis itself takes about a week.

Yes. Those are my primary markets but I also work with clients in Saudi Arabia, Canada, Australia, India, and Pakistan. The methodology is the same, the language, citation sources, and search behaviors shift by market. Multilingual SEO (English plus Arabic, Urdu, or other scripts) is in scope.
